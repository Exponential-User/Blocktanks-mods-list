Instructions for downloading, Inserting, Saving, and opening Developer Tools.

1. Download the zip folder from github

2. when finished downloading extract the contents of the zip folder

3. Go to blocktanks.net

4. Click on the three dots in the top right corner of the screen

5. Click on "More Tools"

6. Click on "Developer Tools"

7. Click on the "Source" tab

8. Click on the "Overrides" tab

9. Click on "Select folder for overrides"

10. Click on the "ThemeAssetReplacer" folder

11. Click select folder

12. Save all files that have the * in front of them (With ctrl+s on devtools while in source tab and overrides still)

13. Refresh the page (With ctrl+r or F5)

14. Enjoy the mod!
